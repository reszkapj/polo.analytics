Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Polo Analytics ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: When registering, we collect your email address and password, or your public profile information (name, email) via Google Sign-In.
  • Horse Profile Photos: Users may upload images of their horses. These images are stored securely and are not shared externally.
  • Location Data: We collect real-time GPS location during tracking sessions to calculate distance, speed, and duration. Location access is required for app functionality.
  • Tracking Data: We measure and record horse movement data including:
    • Maximum speed
    • Total distance
    • Duration of each session

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used to:

  • Enable key features like live tracking and performance statistics
  • Display and analyze horse performance over time
  • Improve the quality and functionality of the application

3. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption and access controls. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data is only shared if required by law or to provide core services (e.g., secure storage or analytics).

5. Your Rights

  • You can request access to your personal data
  • You can request correction or deletion of your account
  • You can withdraw location permissions at any time (note: this disables tracking)

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request full deletion by emailing us at privacy@poloanalytics.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. All changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the revised policy.

If you have questions or concerns, please contact us at contact@poloanalytics.com.
